The Coffin of Andy and Leyley: free browser-based player and mod loader (TCOAAL)

Play The Coffin of Andy and Leyley in your browser

TCOAAL Browser Player is a free, open-source fan-made loader that lets you play The Coffin of Andy and Leyley (TCOAAL) by Nemlei (Kit9 Studio) directly inside any modern browser on desktop, laptop, phone or tablet. No install, no upload, no account. Your owned copy of the game stays on your device and is decrypted locally by a Service Worker.

Features

  • Play TCOAAL online in Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari.
  • Mobile-friendly: works on Android and iOS browsers with touch controls.
  • Per-mod save slots that persist across sessions.
  • One-click switching between the base game and fan-made overhauls.
  • All fan mods bundled here are free and hosted on GitHub Pages.
  • AGPLv3 open-source, no telemetry, no ads, no tracking.

Available TCOAAL fan mods and translations

Overhauls and major fan stories: The Coffin of Andrew and Renee (TCOAAR) by Graveyard Shift, The Coffin of Andy and July by Anuwugirlisback, The Coffin of Andy and Lili by Cali, The Lost Coffin of Andrew and Ashley by Sanadergigi, The Dubbing of Andy and Leyley by 6ExtraOrdinary9.

Translations available in: Chinese, French, German, Hungarian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Brazilian Portuguese, Russian, Castilian Spanish, Mexican Spanish, Turkish and Ukrainian.

Quality-of-life plugins: Mouse Control, Unlocker, Analog Move, Message Backlog.

Frequently asked questions

Is it free? The browser loader and every fan mod listed above are free. You do need to own a legitimate copy of The Coffin of Andy and Leyley on Steam to import the game files.

Can I play TCOAAL on mobile? Yes: once your game files are imported, the site behaves like a progressive web app and runs on Android and iOS.

Are my saves uploaded anywhere? No. Saves and game files live in your browser's IndexedDB and localStorage only.

Does it work offline? Yes: after the first import the Service Worker serves everything locally.
